Sources

  • Long-run demographic base: Census/Pew decennial race and Hispanic-origin summary, plus Census Working Paper 56 for historical race and Hispanic-origin transition notes.
  • Current geography and age filters: Census Vintage 2025 state demographic characteristics, SC-EST2025-ALLDATA6, 2020-2025.
  • Foreign-born share: Census historical foreign-born population shares, embedded as national anchor points.
  • Real weekly earnings: FRED/BLS series LES1252881600Q, real median usual weekly earnings.
  • Home prices: FRED/S&P CoreLogic Case-Shiller U.S. National Home Price Index, CSUSHPISA.
  • Home price / wage ratio: dashboard-derived from Case-Shiller index divided by real weekly earnings, then indexed.
  • Crime and homicide: FBI Crime Data API national estimates. The series has methodology and reporting caveats.
  • Prime-age male labor force participation: FRED/BLS series LNS11300061.

Overlay metrics are national only. State and age demographic filters remain available, but overlays should be interpreted as national contextual series unless future state-level metric data is added.
