Method and source notes

  • Long-run view: national decennial shares for 1790-2020, plus a 2025 estimate. It is strongest for broad White/Black long-run comparison; finer categories begin only when source support becomes reliable.
  • Current estimates: 2020-2025 Census Vintage 2025 SC-EST2025-ALLDATA6 state dataset. This enables U.S., region, state, and age filtering.
  • Mutually exclusive categories: modern current-estimate views use White not Hispanic, Black not Hispanic, Hispanic any race, Asian not Hispanic, and Other residual. Rows sum to 100.0% after rounding.
  • Other: residual category. Depending on period/source, it includes American Indian/Alaska Native, Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islander, two or more races, some other race, category gaps, and rounding differences.
  • Markers: legal/census-policy reference points, not causal claims by themselves. They are numbered on the chart and listed below it to avoid label collisions.
  • Zoom: use the zoom buttons, pan buttons, or Ctrl/⌘ + mouse wheel over the chart. Shift + mouse wheel pans the time window.
