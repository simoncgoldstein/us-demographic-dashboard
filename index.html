Sources

  • Long-run demographic base: Census/Pew decennial race and Hispanic-origin summary, plus Census Working Paper 56 for historical race and Hispanic-origin transition notes.
  • Current geography and age filters: Census Vintage 2025 state demographic characteristics, SC-EST2025-ALLDATA6, 2020-2025.
  • Foreign-born share: Census historical foreign-born population shares, loaded from data/overlays.json.
  • Real weekly earnings: BLS/FRED real median usual weekly earnings anchors, loaded from data/overlays.json.
  • Home prices: S&P/CoreLogic Case-Shiller U.S. National Home Price Index anchors, loaded from data/overlays.json.
  • Home price / wage ratio: dashboard-derived from the home-price and wage anchors.
  • Crime and homicide: FBI/UCR national estimate anchors, loaded from data/overlays.json. The series has methodology and reporting caveats.
  • Prime-age male labor force participation: BLS/FRED prime-age male LFPR anchors, loaded from data/overlays.json.

Overlay metrics are national contextual series. State and age demographic filters remain available, but overlays should be interpreted as national context unless state-level metric data is later added.

Overlay anchors are loaded from data/overlays.json so the dashboard does not depend on browser-side FRED/FBI requests at runtime.
