U.S. demographic dashboard

Demographic lines with an optional dotted national overlay. The overlay uses a right-side scale and is for timing/context, not causation.

Sources

  • National long-run demographic data comes from the preserved Census/Pew dashboard payload in the repository.
  • Current geography and age filters use the preserved Census Vintage 2025 state characteristics payload.
  • Overlay anchors load from data/overlays.json, avoiding browser-side calls to outside APIs.
  • Home prices use Case-Shiller national home price index anchors. Real earnings and prime-age male labor-force participation use BLS/FRED-derived anchors. Foreign-born share uses national historical anchor points.
  • The home price / wage ratio is derived inside the dashboard from the home-price and wage anchors.
